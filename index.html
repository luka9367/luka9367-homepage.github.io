About

I'm a soon-to-be M.phil student at the School of Science and Engineering, CUHK(SZ), where I'll start in September 2026 after graduating with a B.Sc. in Mathematics & Applied Mathematics from China University of Mining and Technology (GPA 91.74 / 100, top of the cohort).

My path so far has been deliberately cross-disciplinary: pure math at the undergraduate level (mathematical analysis 97, advanced algebra 99, mathematical statistics 97, real analysis 97), an early research stint in numerical optimization that produced a paper in Networks and Spatial Economics (Springer, JCR Q3), and a self-directed pivot to applied AI after a summer research internship at CUHK(SZ) in 2025.

Today, I split my time between two surfaces: the engineering of multi-modal generative systems (turning free, open-weight models like Zhipu CogView-3-Flash into reproducible academic figure pipelines) and the product surface of AI tools (translating user needs into design specs, as I did at the Tencent Future PM Camp and during the China Mobile AI internship).

Outside of work, I've been a provincial-level math olympiad winner, built a knowledge-graph ODE learning system as part of a national innovation program, and spent a summer in Singapore at NTU on a Jiangsu provincial government overseas scholarship.
